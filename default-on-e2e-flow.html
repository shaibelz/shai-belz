Step 1 — Pre-Notice Email
Merchant receives advance notice
~2 weeks before activation, the merchant receives an email explaining that Multi Currency with auto display will be enabled on their site.
Multi Currency is coming to your site
From: Wix Payments <noreply@wix.com>

We're enabling Multi Currency on your site

Starting [date], your cross-border visitors will automatically see prices in their local currency. This has been shown to increase checkout conversion by up to 9%.

A currency conversion fee may apply on converted transactions, depending on your payment provider.

Want to enable it now? Go to your Multi-Currency settings and turn it on today.

Prefer to opt out? You can opt out anytime from your Multi-Currency settings page. If you opt out before [date], it won't be enabled on your site.
