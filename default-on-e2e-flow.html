Ongoing — Merchant Controls
Full control at all times
The merchant always has control over their Multi Currency experience. These actions are all available from the BM settings page.
🔄
Turn off auto display
Disable auto display from BM. Must keep at least auto display or converter for MC to work.
➕
Add converter widget
Optionally add the currency converter via the Editor. Works alongside or instead of auto display.
⛔
Deactivate MC entirely
Turn off Multi Currency completely. Buyers go back to seeing site currency only.
💰
Manage currencies
Choose which currencies to support. 45 currencies available by default.
