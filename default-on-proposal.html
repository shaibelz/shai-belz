Multi Currency — Default ON

It's Time to Test the Real Default Experience

We ran a controlled experiment on ~400 Multi Currency sites. Results now show significant conversion uplift across the entire population. But 345K visitors isn't enough to know for sure. The only way to validate the real impact is to go Default ON — and test at real scale.

01 — What We Tested
Auto-Display on a Small, Controlled Audience
12-week A/B test on ~400 active Multi Currency sites with <30% organic traffic. Cross-border visitors split 50/50: Group A saw the merchant's currency, Group B saw their local currency auto-displayed via GeoIP.
