MC requires either Auto Display or Converter
You cannot enable Multi Currency without at least one display method. For Default ON merchants, auto display is the default. For existing merchants, the converter already fulfills this.
Auto Display and Converter are independent
A merchant can have both, either, or (if MC is off) neither. Adding or removing the converter does not affect auto display. They live side by side.
Supported provider is a prerequisite
MC only works with Stripe, PayPal, or Wix Payments. Merchants without a supported provider cannot be flipped on and don't receive the pre-notice email.

Currency Converter is always independent. It can be added or removed at any time via the Editor, regardless of auto display state. Adding the converter doesn't turn off auto display. Removing the converter doesn't turn off auto display. They are separate features that both serve Multi Currency.
