Scenario 1 — Auto Display ON (default)
This is the default experience for merchants who get Multi Currency turned on via the Default ON rollout. Auto display is active out of the box. The converter is shown as an optional add-on they can install if they want. No required steps.

Scenario 2 — Auto Display Available (opt-in)
For merchants who already have Multi Currency but haven't been defaulted into auto display. They see it as a new feature they can turn on. The converter widget remains as a separate optional item.

Scenario 3 — Both Auto Display + Converter ON
For merchants who have both auto display active and the converter widget installed. Both features work together. To remove the converter, the merchant goes to the Editor — it's a site widget, not a BM toggle.
