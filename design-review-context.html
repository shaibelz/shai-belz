The Solution — Auto Display for the buyer.
Zero setup for the merchant.

Detect the buyer's location, display their local currency automatically. No merchant configuration needed — which means we can turn this on for everyone. Auto display is the standard on Shopify, WooCommerce, and many others.

The Constraint

SEO won't allow silent auto-display

We can't switch the currency instantly on page load — Google would see different content per location, which is a cloaking risk.

SEO requires a notification-based approach: the page loads in the merchant's currency, then after ~5 seconds, a notification informs the buyer and switches to their local currency. The buyer can keep or revert.
